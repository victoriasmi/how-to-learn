Main problems with learning things

Neither at school nor at the institute do they teach us how to study the material correctly. We prepare for exams, we train ourselves to solve repetitive problems in order to do better at test, but often in the end this does not give us real knowledge. Results of cramming wear out quickly and are of no future use.

Conclusion: you also need to learn how to learn, but for some reason no one teaches us. So what do we do with that?

There are specific techniques and exercises help to change the approach to learning, make it effective and exciting.

Study techniques

5 practices by Barbara Oakley

  • 2 types of attention

    Deep learning happens if you switch between focused and diffused thinking

  • Recall

    Recall what you learned - it helps to connect different pieces of memory.

  • Interliving

    Learn several skills at the same time, they enrich each other.

  • Questions

    As you listen to your teacher, come up with a good question that you really care about.

  • Illusion of competence

    Repeat, write it down, tell a friend: it only seems that you know the topic.
